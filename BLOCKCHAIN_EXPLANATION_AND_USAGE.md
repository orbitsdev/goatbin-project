# BLOCKCHAIN

## 1. What is Blockchain?

**Simple definition:**

> Blockchain is a **digital record book** that **cannot be changed or deleted** once data is written.

**Another way to say it:**

> Blockchain = **Permanent storage** where data is **WRITE + READ only** (no edit, no delete)
